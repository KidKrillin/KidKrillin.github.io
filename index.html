FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

HOW LONG DOES THE WHOLE PROCESS TAKE?

Most sales can be completed in as little as 7–14 days, depending on how quickly title work and paperwork come back. We state up to 21 days to account for any unexpected title issues that can be easily resolved. If you need more time, we can usually work around your schedule and close when you’re ready.

IS SUBMITTING MY INFORMATION TO YOU BINDING?

No, reaching out is completely risk-free. Submitting your information does not lock you into anything. You’re just getting an offer and exploring your options, whether you accept is entirely up to you.

WHAT IF I STILL HAVE A LOAN ON MY HOME?

That’s not a problem. We’ll pay off your existing mortgage as part of the closing process. Any money left over after paying the loan balance and closing costs goes directly to you.

WILL I NEED TO PAY FEES OR COMMISSIONS?

No. Unlike selling through a realtor, you won’t pay commissions or hidden fees when you work with us. In most cases, we even cover standard closing costs. The number we agree on is the amount you’ll receive at closing.

CAN I SELL IF I’M FACING FORECLOSURE?

Absolutely. In many cases, we can work quickly enough to stop the foreclosure process, pay off what you owe, and help you avoid long-term damage to your credit. Every situation is unique, but the sooner you reach out, the more options we’ll have.

WHAT MAKES YOU DIFFERENT FROM LISTING WITH A REALTOR?

Selling to us is faster and simpler. You don’t pay commissions, don’t have to show your home to multiple buyers, and don’t have to wait months for a sale. Realtors can often get you top market price, but it comes with time, repairs, showings, and fees. We focus on speed, convenience, and certainty.
